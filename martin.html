The Interactive Tree
Design Direction — March 2026

The Idea

A living 3D pomegranate tree that the user explores by rotating it with a swipe. Five fruits hang on the branches — each one a pillar of the platform: Song, Voice, Economy, Community, Technology.

The tree is both the map and the metaphor. The pomegranate — the Granat — is literal: a fruit that cracks open to reveal the richness inside.

The Experience

Fruits glow and reveal their name only when facing the camera — creating a natural sense of discovery as the user rotates the tree. Tap a fruit — the tree gently fades, the pomegranate stays, and the section page appears beneath it.

Two paths: free exploration at your own pace, or "Guide me" for a curated walk through every section. The "Leave your voice" button is always present — a direct invitation to participate.

Design Direction

"A living digital artifact you want to join." Not a website, not a pitch deck. An organic, breathing experience. Mobile-first — designed to be shown from a phone, one hand, one tap, one voice.

The aesthetic: morning light through a cathedral window. Warm, sacred, inviting. Light erupts from within — every element glows, pulses, lives.
